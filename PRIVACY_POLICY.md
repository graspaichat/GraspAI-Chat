# Privacy Policy for GraspAI Chat

*Last updated: April 2026*

## Overview

GraspAI Chat ("the app") is a native iOS client for AWS Bedrock. This privacy policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device
- **AWS Credentials** — Your AWS Access Key, Secret Key, API Key, or Cognito configuration are stored exclusively in the iOS Keychain on your device. They are never transmitted to our servers.
- **Conversation History** — Your chat messages and conversations are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. They are not uploaded to any server we operate.
- **Generated Images** — Images created through the app are stored in your device's local Documents directory and optionally saved to your photo library with your permission.
- **App Settings** — Your preferences and configuration are stored locally on your device.

### Data Transmitted to Third Parties
- **AWS Bedrock** — Your messages and any attached images are sent directly to AWS Bedrock via the AWS SDK using your own AWS credentials. This data is governed by the [AWS Privacy Notice](https://aws.amazon.com/privacy/).
- **RevenueCat** — Subscription status and purchase information are managed by RevenueCat. See the [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).
- **Apple** — Payment processing for subscriptions is handled by Apple. See the [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not operate any servers that receive your data
- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not collect crash reports
- We do not share your data with advertisers
- We do not store your AWS credentials anywhere outside your device

## Permissions

- **Camera** — Used only to capture photos you choose to attach to messages. Photos are sent to AWS Bedrock as part of your message only when you explicitly send them.
- **Photo Library** — Used to select photos to attach to messages, and to save generated images when you choose to do so.

## Children's Privacy

GraspAI Chat is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
